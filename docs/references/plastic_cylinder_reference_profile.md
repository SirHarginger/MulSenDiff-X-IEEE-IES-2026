# Plastic Cylinder Inspection Profile Reference

## Defect pattern
Plastic-cylinder inspections may produce compact detector highlights on otherwise normal parts when shape alignment or local appearance varies within the expected range.

## Sensor signature
Look for compact cross-modal inconsistency with weak thermal and geometric support. If the anomaly does not correspond to visible material damage, treat it as a normal-profile verification case first.

## Likely causes
- Benign capture misalignment
- Normal reflective or contour variation
- Small local contrast shift without true structural damage

## Inspection checks
- Compare the highlighted region with a known normal plastic cylinder
- Confirm that the compact cue does not correspond to a crack, hole, or missing material
- Review whether the same region recurs across multiple normal samples

## Corrective action
Keep the sample as monitored-normal if no physical damage is visible. Review placement and baseline normalization if the same compact cue appears frequently.

## Trust notes
This profile is intended for detector signals that require a normal-reference comparison before escalation.
